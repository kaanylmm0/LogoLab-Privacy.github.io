# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## 1. Introduction

AI Logo Maker ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

## 2. Data We Collect

- **Account information:** Email address and authentication data via Firebase Auth
- **Usage data:** App interactions and analytics via Firebase Analytics
- **Images:** Photos you select from your device gallery for logo creation
- **Payment information:** Subscription and purchase data for Pro plan (processed securely via Google Play)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- To provide and improve our logo creation service
- To manage your account and Pro subscription
- To generate AI-powered logos using OpenAI API
- To send important app updates

## 4. Third-Party Services

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication and analytics — [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **OpenAI:** AI image and logo generation — [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Google Play:** Payment processing — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

## 6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
